Model overview

This model adopts deep learning technology to deal with the complex recognition and classification work of diabetic retinopathy.

Social current situation

Diabetic retinopathy is the main cause of new cases of blindness among diabetic patients. In 2020, more than 103 million diabetic patients worldwide were affected by diabetic retinopathy. It is projected that this number will increase to 160 million by 2045. Compared with all other major causes of blindness, diabetic retinopathy was the only disease whose age-standardized prevalence did not decline between 1990 and 2020.

Technical details

By combining the multi-layer convolutional structure of VGG and the residual layer of ResNET, this model is able to identify DR at different stages.

APTOS 2019 Blindness Detection Dataset

The APTOS dataset is an image dataset used for the detection of diabetic retinopathy. Diabetic retinopathy is one of the main causes of blindness in adults of working age. The APTOS dataset is provided by Aravind Eye Hospital in India and aims to assist in the detection and prevention of this disease, especially in rural areas where medical screening is difficult.
